Benefits of Weight Gain:

  • Improves Strength & Muscle Mass – Helps build muscle, increasing physical strength and endurance.
  • Boosts Energy Levels – Provides sufficient fuel for daily activities and workouts.
  • Enhances Immune System – Adequate nutrition supports a stronger immune response.
  • Promotes Better Recovery – Helps in faster healing and muscle repair after injuries or workouts.

Tips of Weight Gain:

  • Eat More Calories – Consume more calories than you burn daily.
  • Increase Protein Intake – Helps in muscle growth and recovery.
  • Eat Nutrient-Dense Foods – Focus on whole grains, nuts, dairy, and healthy fats.
  • Eat More Frequently – Have small meals throughout the day.
  • Include Healthy Carbs – Whole grains, potatoes, and starchy vegetables help.
  • Stay Consistent – Gradual, steady weight gain is healthier.
